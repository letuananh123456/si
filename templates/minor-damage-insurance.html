What does my SMART policy cover?

Please see the diagram for eligible areas.

  • Scratches, scuffs and dents up to 300mm in length and 3mm deep
  • Stone chips up to 5mm in diameter and 1.5mm deep
  • Covers vulnerable panels including front and rear valances
  • Professionally repaired to immaculate standards

How it works

Whether it’s a paint, dent or wheel repair, our Shine!® technicians have the professional lifetime guaranteed solution.

  • Body Panel Dents
  • Body Panel Scratches

Please remember to add a photo to your claim before you submit it to us
Take a few photographs of each damaged area; at least one from close up and one from further away showing the whole panel(s) where the damage is located.

Place a ruler, measuring tape, or the long side of an A4 piece of paper next to the damage to show us the length/size of the damaged area.

Where there are multiple damages in the same area of the car we will need a photograph showing the whole panel. It may be helpful to place sticky notes next to each area of damage.

Please name all photographs to enable us to cross match each photograph to the damaged area they relate to.
