About Us

At SMART, we have a clear business philosophy of delivering value for each of our business partners. All products are geared towards helping you grow your business and treat your customers fairly.

Whether it’s a SMART branded product that sits in your showroom or a white-labelled, bespoke package tailored to your brand, our class-leading products provide new and significant revenue opportunities that help to grow your business by driving customer loyalty and retention.

At our heart is a strong set of values that outline our commitment to doing business honestly and fairly. These are: Authenticity, Innovation, Trust, Passion, Inclusivity and Quality. By living and breathing these values we provide a refreshing and transparent approach in the F&I automotive space. Our unrivalled standard of service has seen us grow to become the UK’s leading provider of minor damage insurance, paint protection and other VAPs.

SMART Insurance products always deliver competitive returns, industry-leading value and great customer outcomes.
